Elastic electron scattering experiment on Ca40, Ca42, Ca44, Ca48, Ti48 at incident energies of 250 MeV and 500 MeV with
angles between 30 degrees and 125 degrees (0.7 fm-1<q<2.3 fm-1). Absolute cross sections are only provided for Ca40 -
all other nuclides are reported as cross section ratios. Parabolic Fermi distributions are fit to the data, although
uncertainties in extracted quantities are not reported. Charge distribution differences are also given, but are again
without uncertainties. The exception is the radius of Ti40, which is reported with a 0.04 fm uncertainty. This work is
utilized by later papers (see Bergem 1988) as a source of data for V factors and V factor differences.
