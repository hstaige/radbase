Muonic X-ray measurements of the K and L transitions for Hf176-180. Transition energies are provided for the even-A
isotopes, along with the intensities of a deformed Fermi distribution fit. The charge distribution parameters, along
with some lowlying matrix elements and isomeric energies, are included in the fit of the spectrum. Additional nuclear
polarization corrections are addressed by adding a large uncertainty (<600 eV for K transitions, <250 eV for L
transitions) to transition energies and derived Barrett radii. Given the low uncertainty for the derived charge radii (0.002 fm - 0.005 fm),
it seems likely the extracted radii do not include these additional uncertainties. This is suggested by footnote b of
Table V. Other quantities (quadrupole moments, isomeric shifts) are extracted as well.
