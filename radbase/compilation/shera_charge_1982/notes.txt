Muonic atom experiment studying Ba134, 135, 136, 137, 138, with 2p3/2-1s1/2 and 3d5/2-2p3/2 transition energies included.
Transition energies for the odd isotopes are HF CoM. The parameters of a 2pF charge distribution were determined by
fitting the K and L spectra directly. QED and NP corrections were calculated but not included in the text. Listed radii
and Barrett radii uncertainties are statistical only to allow for the computation of isotope shift errors.
