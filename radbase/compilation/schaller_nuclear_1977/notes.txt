Muonic atom experiment paper studying natural abundance F, Na, Al, Si, P, S, and K samples. Fine structure COM energies
from the np-1s transition are reported for all samples plus the np-2s transitions for K, with precisions on the order of
10 eV. Energies are generally dominated by calibration and systematic uncertainties, and it may be possible to estimate
the correlations present using the calibration lines listed. Theoretical uncertainties come from 10% of lamb shift and
20% of nuclear polarization corrections. The data was fit with a 2pF distribution with fixed t=2.3, and charge radii
and Barrett radii are listed. Interestingly, the data was weighted with the statistical uncertainties only, but the
uncertainty in the charge distribution comes from the total experimental uncertainty.
