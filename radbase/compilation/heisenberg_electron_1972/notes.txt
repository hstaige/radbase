A follow-up to Frosch1968 that measured the cross section ratios of Ti46/Ti48 and Ti48/Ti50 at energies of 198 MeV and
298.5 MeV (0.5 [1/fm] < q <2.5 [1/fm]). Absolute cross sections are obtained by chaining the Ca40 and Ca48/Ca40
measurements of Frosch1968 and then fitting a parabolic Fermi distribution using a partial-wave calculation. Presumably,
 radiative corrections have been applied. Reported values for D_AA' are in units of percent despite not being indicated
 as such. No uncertainties are given on radii, but the authors suggest using the difference in the analysis between
 their 198 MeV only fit and the 198 MeV + 298.5 MeV fit as a rough estimate of the error.
