A muonic atom experiment paper with 2p-1s transition energies and 2p3/2-1s1/2 transition energy differences for Fe54,
56-58, Co59, Ni58,60-64, Cu63,65, and Zn64,66,68,70. The analysis follows a two-step procedure, where the transition
energy shifts are first optimized in a generalized least squares procedure with a reduced chi-squared of 1.1 and 1.2 for
the 2p3/2-1s1/2 and 2p1/2-1s1/2 transitions, respectively. Then, these shifts were fixed, and the absolute energies
were optimized by allowing the transition energy of Fe58 to vary. Barrett radii were extracted from the absolute
transition energies, with Barrett radii shifts also being reported. Only model-dependent radii are given, with no
associated uncertainty. See Wohlfahrt et al.'s 1980 paper (Systematics of nuclear charge distributions ...) for a
reanalysis using electron scattering data.
