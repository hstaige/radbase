Muonic atom experiment and theory paper with a combined analysis with electron scattering data. 2p-1s transition
energies and transition energy differences are reported for K39,41, Ca40,42-44,46,48, Sc45, Ti46-50, V51, Cr50-54, Mn55,
and Fe56. Barrett radii and moments are reported as well as difference of Barrett radii and rms radii. When possible,
rms radii differences were determined in a model independent fashion using the electron scattering cross sections from
Heisenberg72, Lightbody83, and Frosch68. Absolute, model independent radii were only found for Ca40 and Cr52. All other
values in Table VIII are determined from radius shifts relative to these nuclides. The major source of uncertainty in
the radii was the variation of R with the cutoff radius in the FB expansion. Related to Sh76, Wo80, and Li83.
