Muonic atom experiment paper studying natural abundance F, Na, Al, Si, P, S, and K samples. Fine structure COM energies
from the np-1s transition are reported for all samples, plus the np-2s transitions for K, with precisions on the order
of 10 eV. Energies are generally dominated by calibration and systematic uncertainties, and it may be possible to
estimate the correlations present using the calibration lines listed. Theoretical uncertainties come from 10% of the
Lamb shift and 20% of nuclear polarization corrections. The data were fit with a 2pF distribution with fixed t=2.3, and
charge radii and Barrett radii are listed. Interestingly, the data were weighted with the statistical uncertainties
only, but the uncertainty in the charge distribution comes from the total experimental uncertainty.
